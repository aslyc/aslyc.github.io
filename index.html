About me

Hi! My name is Yuchen Li. I am currently a senior researcher at Baidu Search Science, collaborating closely with Dr. Dawei Yin and Dr. Shuaiqiang Wang. I am also an adjunct researcher in Intelligent Network and Trusted Computing Laboratory at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, collaborating with Prof. Linghe Kong. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Linghe Kong (Cheung Kong Scholar) and Prof. Guihai Chen (IEEE Fellow, NSFC Distinguished Young Scholar). From 2021 until my graduation, I served as a research intern in Big Data Laboratory at Baidu Inc., under the mentorship of Dr. Haoyi Xiong and Prof. Dejing Dou. I have published research papers in premium conferences and journals, and have been the recipient of three best paper awards or nominations, including ICDM, DSAA and PRICAI.
Research interests:
My research mainly focuses on data-driven intelligent computing for interconnected systems. Applications include information retrieval, large language models, AI for science, recommender systems, and mobile communication networks.
Prospective Interns:
I am looking for highly self-motivated research or applied interns (PhD or Master students) to join our Baidu Search Science team in Beijing! If you are interested in working with me in the area of information retrieval, data mining and large language models, please email liyuchen02 {at} baidu.com.
